Dear User,

According to GDPR and Google Developer Program Policies, in order to continue with the service of the application, you need to agree with the following usages of your personal data.

Data Stored in Your Local Machine

Following data will be stored in your current machine (and current Chrome browser only):

  • Data usage agreement
  • Software settings for notification
  • Preset personal data used for auto-filling

Data Sent to Our Server

  • Your notification ID
  • Your option for New or Renewal appointment
Your notification ID and preference will be used to send you notification. The notifications will be sent through Google notification services indirectly.

Data Received from Our Server

  • Notifications of appointments
  • Emergency updates of appointment related policy changes

Data Received from Third Parties

  • Current available appointments from INIS Registration Appointment System
  • Facebook statics components

Data Used by Third Parties

  • Your application behaviour will send to Facebook while opening the application (Facebook Data Policy).
Facebook will send unidentifialbe data to us as the developer of the applicaiton for statistics purpose such as users from where, of which ages use our application more, when there will be more users using our application, etc. Such data would be used and shown on home page of GNIBIRPVisa.
We won't be able to identify any individual.

Simply uninstall the applicaiton will remove the local data and lead to data on our server invalid.

By agreeing above data usage requirements you'll be able to continue using the applicaiton. Any change of the data usage policies might lead to a new agreement and the old agreement will be considered due while new agreement replaced.
